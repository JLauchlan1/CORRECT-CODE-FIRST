A Taste of Jordan

Turtle Green

Turtle green has a truly unique vibe, from the striking décor to the turtle tank - complete with green turtles of course!. A café with good music and a large collection of loose-leaf teas and cakes, its the perfect place to take a break from the hustle and bustle of Amman life.

Bab al-Yemen

Serving a wide-range of traditional Arab dishes, Bab-al Yemen is a friendly, lively and affordable place to eat. Famous for its delicious yet simple menu and its gigantic portions of bread, this is one place that's not to be missed!

Sufra

Sufra is a popular restaurant tucked away on Rainbow Street. You can enjoy a medley of Middle-Eastern cuisine inside its beautiful walled-garden complete with palm trees. The Kanafe is a must-try!

Al-Quds

One of the best places for a bite to eat, Al-Quds sells delicious falafel, with good service, and great people. For 1 dinar you can have a fresh sandwhich made with what some would call the best falafel in Amman!

Hashem

Located in bustling down-town Amman, Hashem serves fresh salad, hummus, falafel and bread which can be enjoyed with a view of the nearby markets. Popular with tourists as many of the staff speak English, yet many locals can be seen dining there too - a true mark of its authentic deliciousness.

Habibah Sweets

This traditional Jordanian sweet-shop is renowned for its delicious, homemade sweets. The Kanafe and Muhallabeyeh are rated the best in the city. A perfect shop to find a little taste of the Middle-East for friends and family back home.
